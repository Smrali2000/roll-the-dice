Roll the dice

Objective:
  • The objective of the game is to accurately predict which of two rolled dice will show a higher number.
Rules:
  • A player clicks the "Roll Dice" button to roll both dice simultaneously.
  • Which of the two dice will show a higher number, that player wins the game
Fair Play:
  • Players should agree on the rules and any variations before starting the game.
  • Honesty and integrity are essential; players must take predictions openly and accept the outcome, whether favorable or not.
